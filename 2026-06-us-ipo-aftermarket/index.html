Methodology & Disclosures

  • Data sources. IPO offer prices and deal sizes are sourced from the Nasdaq IPO Calendar; daily closing prices are sourced from Yahoo Finance.
  • Checkpoints are trading days. All horizons (+7 / +15 / +30 / +45) are counted in trading days after the first trading session. Day 0 is the first session, so "+7d" is the close seven trading days later.
  • As-traded prices. Where Yahoo Finance had retroactively split-adjusted a series, prices were restored to their as-traded values so that the offer price and early closes are comparable. This affected tickers.
  • SPAC identification is a heuristic. SPACs are flagged by a combined rule — company-name tokens (Acquisition, Capital Corp, Equity Partners, Merger, SPAC) plus a structural test (unit ticker ending in "U" priced at exactly $10.00) — and are excluded by default; toggle "Include SPACs" to add them back.
  • Backfilled delisted issues. Delisted/acquired companies () absent from the primary source were backfilled from stockanalysis.com; their IPO first-day close is unavailable, so Day-1 Pop shows —.
